Legal:
UK Legal Requirements (Food Safety (Temperature Control) Regulations 1995): Chilled food must be kept at ≤8°C (best practice ≤5°C). Frozen food must be kept at ≤−18°C. Hot food on display must be kept at ≥63°C at all times. Failure to comply is a criminal offence. Temperature records form part of your due diligence defence under the Food Safety Act 1990.
