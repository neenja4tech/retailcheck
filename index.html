Daily Checklists

Temperature Monitoring
AM & PM fridge, freezer and hot-hold checks with photo evidence. Out-of-range alerts. Fully compliant with UK Food Safety (Temperature Control) Regulations 1995.

HACCP Cooking Records
Per-batch core temperature logging, 2-hour rule tracking, discard times and full batch traceability. Landscape PDF export. Built for EHO inspection.

Delivery Records
Log every delivery with supplier, driver, temperature, condition and photos. Accepted / partial / rejected status. Full PDF audit trail.
💷
Daily Cash Report
Photo evidence for Z-reads, banking slips, lottery records and fuel ullage. No manual data entry — just snap and upload. All embedded in the daily PDF.
📊
Monthly Compliance Audit
FSA/FHRS-aligned scoring across Food Safety, Hygiene, Premises, Management and Staff categories. Historical scores with corrective action plan.
📅
Periodic Checks
Weekly, monthly and yearly scheduled checks with overdue alerts. Completion history with timestamps and sign-offs surfaced in the summary dashboard.

History & Analytics
Archive daily reports and reprint any past day as PDF. Weekly, monthly and yearly trend charts — completion rates, temperature alerts, audit scores.
